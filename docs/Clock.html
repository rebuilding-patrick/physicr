    def tick : Float64 #

    Attemps to advance the system clock. Returns delta if time changes, 0 if not Delta is the ratio of time that has passed since the tick. For example if your tick rate is 100ms, and your tick happens 150ms after your last tick, your delta will be 1.5 You probably are going to want to use this one for timing in games
